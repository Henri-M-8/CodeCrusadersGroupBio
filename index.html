Our Mission Statement

We strive to create a collaborative, inclusive, and innovative environment where team members feel empowered to push boundaries and achieve excellence in their craft. Our mission is to deliver high-quality solutions that meet and exceed our clients' expectations.

Team Rules

  • Commitment: The team will commit to achieving its stated goals and will handle falling behind schedule principally by preventing such occurrences by holding a sync-up meeting to verify the progress and evaluate if a high-quality “Done” increment was delivered that particular week, and adjust and adapt accordingly.
  • Courage: We will agree to do the right thing, especially when tackling tough problems, act according to our judgment, and not be afraid to challenge the status quo if we deem it fruitful towards achieving our objectives. This includes trying things outside of traditional methods.
  • Focus: We will concentrate on the work discussed and agreed upon during the sync-up every week, and not get distracted and add on work that has not been agreed upon.
  • Openness: We will agree and encourage each other to foster transparency about the work we are doing with each other. This will also facilitate dealing with an uncooperative team member when we have as much information as possible to work with, and to show them the goodwill in each of our concerted efforts to improve the product.
  • Respect: We recognize each team member as capable and independent, and afford a degree of autonomy to everyone by valuing different expertises and perspectives. It is very important to answer other group members' questions and not take it as a sign that they are not capable.
